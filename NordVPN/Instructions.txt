Guide for setting up OpenVPN with NordVPN

Create an account with Nord VPN


Go here https://raw.githubusercontent.com/davesayers2014/OpenVPN/master/NordVPN/VPN.sh copy all the text and paste in note pad.


We only need to edit lines 1 and 2

Change line 1 uuuu with your username between the "" 

Example USERNAME='davesayers19'

Change line 2 pppp with your password between the "" 

example PASSWORD='149356d'


Once you have edited your details talnet/putty the script and reboot box, now goto plugins VPN Changer
